Jeff Doan; a bit about me.

A lover of garage rock and minivans; a product designer and front-end tinkerer; a husband and a rad dad with 15% more mohawk. I also love Tolkein and Discworld; wiener dogs; and my collection of colorful pants.

To me, product design is not about making beautful things. Effective product design is about creating something that you truly believe in; something human beings will, and want to, use; and making it better and better everyday.

The word beautiful permeats the digital product world unfortunately; and it does nothing to help me, nor anyone else, derive any significant value about said product.
